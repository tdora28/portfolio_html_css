About me

I studied linguistics at the university, and later decided to go after a new challenge. I started learning web development in 2022. At first I was just curious but after a while I realized that building websites is a really good fit for me. I like creating new things (that's why I love drawing too) and I'm a very organized person. Coding also makes it possible for me to become a librarian - my childhood dream. Interacting with APIs is like handling books in a library. Contact me if you'd like to build something awesome together!
